V. V.

Front-End Dev

My name is Viktorija Valsø. I'm a Norwegian-French Front-End Developer living in Stockholm. I am a vivid technology and design enthusiast, who is specially interested in the interception of those two disciplines.

Most of all i enjoy imagining and building user-friendly beautiful "internets".
I offer my design skills to build awesome websites and mobile applications.  I my toolbox I’ve got JavaScript, JQuery, React, PHP, HTML5, CSS, SASS, Wordpress, Sketch, Figma & Adobe Creative Suite. With those I am able to solve most of challenges with the exception of making Comic Sans font look sophisticated.

What I love above all things is pushing the limits of ergonomics and conception without ever neglecting user experience. Let's work on great projects together.
